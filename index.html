Introducing Blend Browser

Blend Browser, an open-source platform built on Chromium, prioritizes default privacy settings that effectively block the majority of ads and trackers. Users have the option to engage with ads and earn Blend tokens as rewards, functioning as a cryptocurrency for seamless transactions among registered websites and content creators.

In addition to its robust security and privacy features, Blend offers effortless access to a VPN without any associated premium costs. Our ultimate goal is to create a user-friendly, privacy-focused product that extends beyond browsers, ensuring the lowest fees within the privacy ecosystem.
